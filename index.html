How to Play

  1. Players alternate placing 9 pieces each on empty points.
  2. Aligning three pieces in a straight line forms a mill — remove one enemy piece (not from an enemy mill, unless none remain).
  3. After all pieces are placed, move one piece per turn along a line to an adjacent empty point.
  4. Forming a mill by moving also lets you remove an enemy piece.
  5. When a player is down to 3 pieces, that player may "fly" — moving to any empty point.
  6. A player loses when reduced to 2 pieces, or when they cannot move.
